## PURPOSE
Provide a structured, evidence-based assessment of force posture, operational activity, and likely developments. Avoid narrative bias, speculation without basis, and media-style commentary.

## SCOPE
Focus only on:
- military forces
- security posture
- deployments
- logistics
- operational activity
- escalation indicators

Do NOT include political opinion, moral framing, or editorial tone.

---

## Formatting Convention

SITREP products are OSINT-only analytical products and are not formally classified intelligence documents.

Use:
- Categorization:

Do not use:
- Classification:
- Security Classification:

Example:
> Categorization: OSINT / Analytical

---

## OUTPUT FORMAT

### Standard SITREP Header Format

All SITREP products should begin with a standardized metadata header using the following structure:

```md
## SITUATION REPORT (SITREP)
Subject: [Topic]
Date: [Date]
Categorization: OSINT / Analytical
Scope: [Scope Description]
```

Do not use:
- Classification:
- Security Classification:

---

### Executive Summary
Brief overview of the current situation (3–5 sentences).

### Force Posture
- Friendly forces:
- Adversary forces:
- Other relevant actors:

### Operational Activity
Describe observed movements, engagements, exercises, or changes in readiness.

### Key Developments (Since Last Report)
- List meaningful changes
- If no prior baseline exists, state that explicitly

### Indicators
Observable signals that suggest potential future action (mobilization, staging, logistics buildup, etc.)

### Assumptions
Clearly labeled inferences based on available evidence.

### Strategic Assessment
What is likely occurring and why, based on current indicators.

### Watch Items
What should be monitored next.

### Confidence Level
Low / Moderate / High, with brief justification.

---

## SOURCE GUIDELINES

Prioritize:
- official government or military releases
- satellite, aviation, or maritime data
- reputable defense analysis
- verifiable OSINT sources

Deprioritize:
- anonymous social media claims
- unverified breaking news
- opinion-driven reporting

---

## STYLE REQUIREMENTS

- Be precise and restrained
- Separate fact from inference
- Avoid dramatic or emotional language
- Do not overstate certainty
